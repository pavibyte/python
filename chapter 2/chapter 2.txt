CHAPTER 2 PYTHON BASICS

VARIABLES:

     can be made up of letters, numbers, or a mixture of two.
     can contain an underscore but must start with the alphabet not even a number.
     python keywords cannot be used as a variable.
     Special symbols cannot be used for variable names.

VALUES:
     
     values are held by the variable.

EXAMPLE: name_001="Pavithra"
         variable=value

EXPRESSION: E.g.:2+2
STRING: collection of character
        always surrounded by quotations. 
        e.g.: "Pavithra"
